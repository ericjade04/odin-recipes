Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Description:

Indulge in a plate of comforting and creamy garlic Parmesan pasta.

This simple yet elegant dish combines al dente pasta with a luscious garlic Parmesan sauce that will tantalize your taste buds.

Follow our easy recipe to create a satisfying meal in no time.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces fettuccine or your preferred pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Procedures

  1. Cooking the Pasta:

    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
    • Drain the pasta and set it aside.

  2. Making the Garlic Parmesan Sauce:

    • In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
    • Add the minced garlic to the skillet and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant, being careful not to burn it.
    • Pour in the heavy cream and stir well. Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
    • Gradually whisk in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce becomes smooth and creamy.
    • Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning according to your preference.

  3. Combining the Pasta and Sauce:

    • Add the cooked and drained pasta to the skillet with the garlic Parmesan sauce.
    • Toss the pasta gently to coat it evenly with the sauce.
    • Continue cooking over low heat for 2-3 minutes, allowing the pasta to absorb some of the sauce and heat through.
    • If the sauce thickens too much, you can add a splash of pasta cooking water to loosen it.

  4. Serving:

    • Transfer the creamy garlic Parmesan pasta to serving plates or bowls.
    • Garnish with fresh chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.
    • Serve immediately as a main dish or alongside a crisp salad and garlic bread for a complete meal.
